Our Heritage

Uncompromising Authenticity in Every Grain

At Bappus Biryani & Arabian Catering, we believe that a true feast is a royal experience. Our chefs masterfully blend hand-picked cardamom, cloves, and premium saffron with premium aged basmati rice and tender, pasture-raised meats. Following generations-old slow-cooking "dum" methods, we craft authentic culinary experiences that carry the warmth of Arabian hospitality.

Traditional Slow Dum Cooking

Hand-sealed pots slow-cooked under gentle charcoal embers to lock in juices and aromatics.

Hand-Picked Premium Spices

Imported saffron, pure ghee, and customized spice mixes ground daily in our kitchens.

Elite Majlis Hospitality

Attentive waiters, traditional Gahwa (Arabic coffee) service, and premium plating.
